Artistic Encounters

June 15-17, 2023

Artistic Encounters, New York City

Experience the Diversity of Contemporary Art

Artistic Encounters is a three-day art show featuring diverse contemporary art styles from around the world. The event provides an opportunity for emerging and established artists to showcase their work, connect with art enthusiasts, and build relationships with art industry professionals.

  • Art Exhibits: Featuring over 100 artists and their artwork.
  • Art Talks: A series of panel discussions and presentations on various art-related topics.
  • Art Workshops: Interactive sessions led by experienced artists, covering a range of art techniques and styles.
  • Date and Time for Each Session: June 15-17, 2023, from 10:00 am to 7:00 pm
  • Brief Session Descriptions: See the full schedule for session details.

Dr. Joseph Nguyen

Joseph is an art historian and professor at a local university. His research focuses on contemporary art and its cultural significance.

Laura Rodriguez:

Laura is an award-winning artist known for her vibrant use of color and abstract expressionist style. Her work has been featured in galleries around the world.

Yuna Lee

Yuna is a mixed media artist who explores themes of identity and belonging in her work. She has exhibited her art in numerous shows and galleries.

  • Acme Art Supplies: Acme is a leading supplier of art materials, offering a wide range of products for artists of all skill levels.
  • Artful Home: Artful Home is an online marketplace featuring handmade art and decor from artists across the US.
  • The Art Fund: The Art Fund is a charity that supports museums and galleries across the UK, helping to make art accessible to everyone.
